POS System Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the POS System application collects, uses, and protects your information while using the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Product inventory data uploaded by users
  • Billing and invoice information
  • Google account information during login
  • Basic app settings and preferences

2. Google Login

This app uses Google Sign-In for authentication. We only access basic profile information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo

We do not access your password. Authentication is securely handled by Google Firebase Authentication.

3. Data Storage

Your inventory, templates, and backup data may be stored securely using Firebase Firestore for synchronization and cloud backup features.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Security

Reasonable security measures are used to protect your data. However, no online system can guarantee complete security.

6. Your Control

You can:

  • Delete inventory data anytime
  • Logout from your account
  • Clear local app data

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected on this page.
